Under the Same Sun

Site Redesign

Under The Same Sun a charity investing significantly within Tanzania to improve the lives of Persons With Albinism.

UTSS came to me with a request to redesign their website. I redesigned their look from scratch and built the site on the Drupal 7 platform. One of the big challenges was converting a large database of articles and resources from their old wordpress site and convert the database to drupal. Along with the main site I also set up an e-commerce system using Ubercart 2 which they use to sell event tickets.

The net result was a large increase in traffic and started their facebook campaign which currently has over 6000 likes on their page.
